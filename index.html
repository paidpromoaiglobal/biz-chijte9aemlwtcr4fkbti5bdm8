Our Story

Made with Passion

A.Ali Krishi Bikash Kendra is a dedicated agricultural development establishment located in Bejorsuti, Rangapani, Joukhata Gaon, Assam. We are committed to empowering local farmers and food producers with the resources, knowledge, and supplies they need to cultivate thriving, productive farms. Our centre serves as a one-stop destination for all agricultural needs in the region, bridging the gap between modern farming practices and the hardworking farming communities of Assam.

Rooted in the rich agricultural heritage of Assam, A.Ali Krishi Bikash Kendra takes pride in supporting sustainable farming and rural development. Whether you are a smallholder farmer, a commercial grower, or simply passionate about food and agriculture, our centre is here to guide and support you every step of the way. With a perfect 5-star reputation built on trust and dedication, we remain committed to the growth and prosperity of our local farming community.
